The Kid's Learning Hub is an application made for young people to learn the basic lessons in an interactive way. It's like an interactive flash cards. It includes awesome graphics and audio that will capture the interest of the audiences.

The developers are students of College of Computer Studies, Columban College, Olongapo City, Philippines. This app is a project for our subject "System Analysis and Design".

We made this with the inspiration of helping young children learn in a fun way. Originally the app is produced for mobile devices so they can enjoy it even if they aren't at school. The original title of this app is Columban College Kids Learning Hub (CCKLH).
